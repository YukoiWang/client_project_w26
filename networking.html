Networking & Building Connections

Build Your Professional Network

Networking is one of the most effective ways to explore career paths, learn about opportunities, and build relationships that support your professional growth. Whether you are new to networking or looking to refine your approach, the UMSI Career Development Office offers resources to help you connect with professionals in your field.

Schedule a career coaching appointment to discuss your networking goals and strategies.

Quick Networking Tips

  • Start with people you know: Professors, classmates, and alumni are great first contacts.
  • Use Career Link: Career Link connects you with employers and alumni interested in UMSI students.
  • Attend CDO events: Attend a career event to meet recruiters and professionals.
  • Follow up: Send a thank-you note within 24–48 hours after a conversation.
